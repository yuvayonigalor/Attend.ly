  1. Once you have successfull installed Attend.ly, the application will run in the background of your computer to monitor student attendance.

  2. You can log into your Attend.ly account to access your Instructor Dashboard, were you'll recieve a summary student attendance.

    • Change a student's status from absent to present, in case a student did not bring a connected device to class, and you would still like to give him credit for attending.
    • Cancel a class
